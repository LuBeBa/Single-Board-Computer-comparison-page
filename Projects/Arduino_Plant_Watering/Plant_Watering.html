About the Project

The Robotic Bartender is a great project to combine programming, 3D-Printing and mechanical engineering with a fun project. It doesn't matter if you're building it just for fun, for yourself or for your next party. All you need to do is follow the video instructions that you can find on the YouTube channel “DIY Machines”. This project requires a 3D printer, if you don't have one either ask a friend or have it printed at a 3D printing service. Have Fun!

How designed this?

The Plant Watering System was designed by "Smart Things DIY" in 2022

Main Parts (More detailed on his Page)

  • Arduino Uno
  • Motor drivers
  • Contact Switch
  • Linear Bearings
  • RGB LED
  • 8mm Steel Rods
  • Stepper Motors
  • Timing Belt and Pulley 2m & 5m
  • 3D-Printed Parts

    • Difficulty Level

    This project is for people who have a little more experience in programming and mechanical engineering. So as a beginner, this project is probably not recommended, you should start with something simple, like you can find on our website.
